# Technical Video
* Usually 30s to 2.5mins, to grab attention

## Options to build:
* Zoom
* Loom
* Panopto / WorkWire (usually in educational setting)
* QuickTime (installed in OS 10 Machine)
* Camtasia (commercial)

## Recommended Format:
* 1080P or higher (up to 4K)
* 720P is OK but can be blurry
